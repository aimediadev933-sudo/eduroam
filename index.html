Project Overview

The TEIN eduroam Project successfully deployed secure, federated Wi-Fi access in eight developing Asian countries, transforming educational connectivity and empowering local National Research and Education Networks to operate as National Roaming Operators.

Project Timeline

July 2018

Video Trainings Launch

Four comprehensive video sessions introduced eduroam architecture, NRO responsibilities, and server configurations to participants across all countries.

August 2018

APAN46 Workshop

Major in-person and remote workshop held in New Zealand with 30+ attendees from 8 countries, covering deployment using Docker and monitoring systems.

Q3-Q4 2018

Local Training Cascade

Institutional workshops conducted to cascade knowledge from APAN46 to home-country institutions and build local expertise.

2018-2019

Infrastructure Deployment

Server and WAP procurement, Docker-based deployment tools development, and establishment of country-specific eduroam portals.
